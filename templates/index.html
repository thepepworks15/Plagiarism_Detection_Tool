How It Works

Our tool uses four powerful algorithms to detect plagiarism

Cosine Similarity

Converts documents to TF-IDF vectors and measures the angle between them. Excellent for overall document comparison.

Jaccard Similarity

Measures the overlap between token sets using intersection over union. Detects copy-paste with minor changes.

N-gram Analysis

Compares overlapping word sequences (trigrams) to identify reordered text and structural similarities.

Semantic Similarity

Uses AI sentence transformers to understand meaning. Catches intelligent paraphrasing that lexical methods miss.
